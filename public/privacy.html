Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Kevin Henderson's portfolio website ("the Website"). I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting any information that may be collected while you browse this site.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it's used, and your rights regarding that information. This policy applies to all visitors of kevinhenderson.dev.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Website, certain information may be automatically collected through basic analytics, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website (if applicable)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General geographic location (country/region level)

Information We Do Not Collect

This website does not:

  • Collect personal identification information through forms
  • Store any contact form submissions on our servers
  • Require user registration or accounts
  • Track individual users across sessions

How We Use Information

The limited information collected is used solely for:

  • Website Improvement: Understanding how visitors interact with the site to improve user experience
  • Traffic Analysis: Monitoring overall traffic patterns and popular content
  • Technical Optimization: Ensuring the website performs well across different devices and browsers

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

Netlify (Hosting)

The Website is hosted on Netlify, which may collect basic server logs including IP addresses for security and performance purposes. For more information, see Netlify's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

Basic analytics may be used to understand website traffic. These analytics are configured to minimize data collection and do not track individual users or store personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Information: Request details about any data associated with your visit
  • Opt-Out: Disable cookies through your browser settings to limit data collection
  • Request Deletion: Ask for removal of any data that may be associated with you

Since this website collects minimal data and does not store personal information, there is typically no personal data to access or delete.

Data Security

While no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, reasonable measures are taken to protect any information collected, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Minimal data collection practices

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the Website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact me through the contact form on the main website or via the social links provided in the footer.
